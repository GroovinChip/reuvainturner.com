Welcome to my website!

Welcome to my website! This is where you can view my resume, learn more about me, and get in touch with me.​ The idea is to exercise my website building skills to provide a dynamic and deep resume, where I’m not constrained to a page or two, and can neatly organize all of the content. Kind of like a glorified business card!

Feel free to take a look at the source code of this site to get a feel for my coding and design styles. I've used HTML, CSS with Bootstrap, JQuery, and a dash of PhP.
